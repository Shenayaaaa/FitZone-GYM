Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What types of training programs do you offer?

At FitZone Fitness Center, we offer a variety of training programs, including Freestyle Training, Cardio Training, Personal Training, Hard Abs, Yoga, Zumba and Aerobics, and Strength Training.

2. How can I sign up for a class or personal training session?

You can sign up for classes or personal training sessions by contacting us via phone at +94 11 2 688 461 or by emailing us at support@fitzone.com. Additionally, you can visit our center at 12 A, Main Road, Kurunegala for more information.

3. Do I need to have prior fitness experience to join?

No prior fitness experience is necessary! Our trainers are skilled in working with individuals of all fitness levels, from beginners to advanced athletes.

4. What should I bring to my first class or training session?

We recommend bringing a water bottle, a towel, and wearing comfortable workout clothing and shoes. If you're attending a yoga class, consider bringing a yoga mat (although we have mats available).

5. Are there any membership options available?

Yes! We offer various membership plans that cater to different needs and budgets. Please contact our front desk or visit our website for detailed information on pricing and membership options.

6. Can I try a class before committing to a membership?

Absolutely! We offer trial classes for new members so you can experience our training programs before making a commitment.

7. What safety measures do you have in place?

The health and safety of our members are our top priorities. We follow strict hygiene protocols, including regular cleaning of equipment and facilities.

8. How do I contact my trainer if I have questions?

You can reach out to your trainer directly after your session or through the contact information provided during your initial consultation.

9. What if I need to cancel or reschedule my session?

If you need to cancel or reschedule a session, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance to avoid any cancellation fees.

10. Do you offer nutrition guidance as part of your programs?

Yes, some of our trainers provide basic nutrition guidance as part of their training programs.
